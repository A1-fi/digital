About me and this Blog

This blog is for an assignment for one of my classes.

I'm a college student in Graphic design, and had no previous coding experience (save for a class I hated in 8th grade.) All in all, I'm enjoying it it, which is unexpected to say the least. Probably going to continue doing it after this class even. Can't say It'll be pretty but I do believe it'll be. I'm curious to see if I can find a way to fit this into how I create art.

I make a lot of things, and unconvential art is one of my favorites. I have no doubt that's what this will devolve into now, knowing the possibilities.

To get more acquainted with my normal art, check the gallery. No quite yet figured out how to put up the more wacky stuff yet.
